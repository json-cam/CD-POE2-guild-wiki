# 10 Things I Wish I Knew Sooner

1. **Spacebar is your friend:** Dodging is a core mechanic now.
2. **Resistances Matter:** Cap your elemental resists before Act 3.
3. **Gold:** Don't hoard it early; use it for gambling or vendor upgrades.

::: info Streamer Tip
*Alkaizer* recommends prioritizing movement speed on boots over almost any other stat during the campaign.
:::
